Who I am

Software development is a family thing here: my uncle and father encouraged me to take it on when I was just nine, which means I've been at this for almost ten years now. I can honestly say that I have enjoyed and continue to enjoy every single second of it: writing software is not a mere assembly line for me—it's a craft. I thrive when the people's lives are simplified or improved thanks to my work.
