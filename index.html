Who I am

It requires dedication and skill to create a polished, functional product that makes people happy. Things shouldn't just work. They should be beautiful. I thrive when the people's lives are simplified or improved thanks to my work.

Why I want to join

I'm looking to join you, and that's for a couple of reasons.

First of all, you have revolutionized the way I write software. I know, I know: Rails is not Ruby. Except it is: without Rails, I would have never known about Ruby. Without Ruby, I would have never discovered that package managers are a thing. I mean, guys, I used to write PHP. We had PEAR. Then we had Symfony and Composer and all of that stuff but, at the end of the day, we were still writing PHP, with all its procedural quirks. Ruby was magic to me, so much so that I struggled to understand it for the first days, but when I got it... oh boy!

Now, I'm not a Rails fanboy. There are many things which I don't like about Rails. I don't agree with everything you guys say—in fact, I strongly disagree with some of it, which is one of the reasons you could use me. I've been involved in open-source projects that deviate from the Rails way of doing things. I'm even the author of such a set of tools (I'd love to hear your thoughts on Pragma, BTW). I have strong opinions, weakly held. I will speak up when I think something can be done better and shut up when you show me that I'm wrong. That brings me to the second reason I'm eager to work at Basecamp.

I love your culture. You speak about Majestic Monoliths at a time where everybody and their dog seem to be using microservices, for everything, all the time. You create tools that let a single full-stack developer write a mobile app with Good Enough performance, while mobile developers cry at the idea of non-native animations. When others sell their products with pretty UIs and useless whistles, you sell it with a 275-words letter from your CEO and well-polished features that your customers actually need. You focus on people, not software. You know that Work Can Wait because you know that, ultimately, our only goal is to live awesome lives.

Let's make 2017 the most awesome summer yet, Basecamp. I'm ready.
